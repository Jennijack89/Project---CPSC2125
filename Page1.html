Leisure Activities

-Find fun activities and reconnect with your family.

Play

Columbus offer's many places where you and your family can join in a friendly compeition or just have fun!

On Friday nights Midtown Coffee hosts Trivia night and every other Saturday night Harvest Coffee Co. hosts a game night sponsored by Columbus Got Game, where you can choose from a variety of board games, join in a friendlygame and meet new people. There are many places that offer billards games, as well as bowling, arcade games, mini golf and more. I've listed a few of the venues below.

  • Hollywood Connection
  • Stars & strikes
  • Bowleros
  • Billards on Broad

Get Active

Looking for an activity that will get your blood pumping? Columbus has 2 trampoline parks and a rock climbing gym where you can defy gravity and jump until your hearts content.

Learn to shoot at Shooters or pick up axe throwing at Stumpy's. Whether on land, ice, or in a rink we have a place for every skater. For those rainy days, the (bowling center) on fort benning offer's an indoor playground the whole family can enjoy.

  • TreadStone
  • Launch Trampoline Park
  • Sky Zone
  • Shooters

Entertainment

Enjoy a night out, get some popcorn and catch a move on the big screen. Columbus has multiple movie theatres that offer different prices and showtimes to meet every families needs.

If you're more of a sports fans, We have 3 semi-professional sports teams to include Ice hockey, Baseball, and arena football with talk of a basketball team coming to our area soon. Come catch a game and enjoy comradery with our adoring fans.

  • AMC Theatres
  • Columbus Lions - Arena Football
  • Columbus River Dragons - Ice Hockey
  • Chatt-a-Hoots - Baseball
